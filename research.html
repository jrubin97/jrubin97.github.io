Criminal Justice Capstone Thesis

Mandatory Sentences for Domestic Violence Perpetrators

Supervising Professor: Dr. Jaclyn Schildkraut
Grade Received: A

In this 18 page thesis, the history and evolution of domestic violence statutes were evaluated, followed by a discussion surrounding alternative approaches to mandatory sentences. Following a thorough literature review, policy recommendations for statutes regarding mandatory sentences were investigated.

Sociology Capstone Thesis

Sexual Crimes: A Sociological Analysis

Supervising Professor: Dr. Tim Delaney
Grade Received: A

In this 28 page thesis, the impact of sexual crimes on victims was explored through a sociological lens, describing the impacts on mental health, social movements surrounding sexual violence, and categories of sexual violence that are recognized within the U.S. legal system. Sociological theories and methodological approaches were then applied to these areas.
